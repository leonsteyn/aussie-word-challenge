❓ How to Play

Guess the hidden Australian word in as few tries as possible!

After each guess, the tiles change colour:

B

Green: Right letter, right spot. Bonzer!

E

Yellow: Right letter, wrong spot. Almost!

X

Grey: This letter is not in the word.

Clues 🔒

You get 3 clues per game. Use them wisely — each clue reduces your score by 15 points.

  • 🔒 Does the word have any repeating letters?
  • 🔒 Does the word have any rare letters (Z, Q, X, J, K, V, W)?
  • 🔒 See the word's definition.

Scoring 🏆

  • Start with 100 points
  • Each extra guess costs 10 points
  • Each clue costs 15 points
  • No points if you don't solve it

Word lengths

Choose 4, 5, 6 or 7 letters. More letters = more guesses!
